Account Access Sprint

The 4-Week Sprint That Replaces 6 Months of BD

By Anas Elimam · 2026

Traditional business development in KSA looks like this: hire a BD person at SAR 25,000 a month, give them 6 months to "build pipeline," hope a deal closes before month 9.

The math doesn't work. By the time the BD person is productive, you've spent SAR 150,000+ and may not have a single closed deal.

A different shape

An Account Access Sprint is 4 weeks. Fixed scope: 10 named target accounts that you choose.

What happens in 4 weeks:

Week 1 — 10 Account Briefs delivered (the intelligence layer).

Week 2 — Custom outreach drafted for each, in 3 tones (formal Arabic, business English, Saudi casual). Warm-path mapping where intros exist.

Week 3. Outreach goes out. Conversations begin.

Week 4. Pipeline review. Where conversations stuck, why. What converted, what didn't, what's next.

What you actually buy

You don't buy 10 closed deals. Anyone promising that is selling something dishonest.

You buy 10 high-quality, warm-warm conversations with named decision-makers at accounts you specifically chose. From those 10, on average, 3 to 5 reach a second meeting and 1 to 2 enter active commercial discussion.

More importantly: you buy a repeatable process. The next 10 accounts work the same way. Your team learns the system.

Why this works in KSA

Saudi enterprise buyers respond to warm-path approaches better than cold ones. Our network plus your network plus 2nd-degree mapping gives most accounts a viable warm path. We don't pretend cold outreach works, we use it only when no warm path exists.
